Integrated Digitized Biocollections (iDigBio)

Analogous to DataOne for museum catalogs in the US.

Mission: "Making data and images of millions of biological specimens available on the web."
Provides: OccurrenceOrSpecimenData
Api documentation: https://github.com/iDigBio/idigbio-search-api/wiki
Identifiers: idigbio uuid, occurrenceID, Yale occurrenceID
