EoL Encyclopedia of Life


Global access to knowledge about life on Earth

http://eol.org/

From Dikow and Agosti (2015):

"the Encyclopedia of Life (EoL) strives to present a species page for each known
taxon summarizing diverse data from disparate online sources. As a data aggregator, it
relies on information being either stored elsewhere and harvested regularly or entered
directly into the EoL database."
